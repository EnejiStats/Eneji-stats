Enejistats

Enejistats is an innovative soccer statistics platform designed to bring deep insight, transparency, and performance tracking to the world of football. Our goal is to give clubs, players, and scouts a powerful tool to monitor, analyze, and compare performance using real-time data.

Whether you're a player looking to improve your game, a coach aiming to optimize your team's strategy, or a scout searching for talent, Enejistats provides the tools you need. Our platform supports player-by-player and team-by-team analysis, live match data collection through our scout widget, and advanced filtering to find the exact insights you're looking for.

With tiered access for CTOs, admins, and data scouts, and dashboards tailored to individual users, we ensure that the right people get the right data, at the right time.

Our mission is to revolutionize how soccer performance is measured and talent is discovered, particularly across grassroots and professional levels where tracking has traditionally been limited.
