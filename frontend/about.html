About Enejistats

Enejistats is an innovative soccer analytics platform designed to empower players, clubs, and scouts with high-resolution match data. From grassroots to elite levels, we help users track, analyze, and compare performance with unmatched accuracy.

Whether you're a player seeking improvement, a coach optimizing your squad, or a scout evaluating talent, Enejistats gives you access to vital data. Analyze passing accuracy, dribbles, duels, shots, and more with full historical and per-match breakdowns.

Our scout widget allows real-time, venue-based stat collection, while club and player dashboards ensure transparency and accountability. Enejistats introduces a tiered access system (CTO, Admin, Scout) for secure, role-based data control.

Through visual summaries and detailed stat tables, players and clubs gain actionable insights into performance trends. We’re revolutionizing talent discovery and game analysis through data — empowering football communities worldwide.

Welcome to the next generation of football intelligence.
